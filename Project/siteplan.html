Site Plan: How to Handle Power Outages in Ecuador

Site Name

Proposed Site Name: Ecuador Power Outage Guide

This name reflects the website's purpose of providing helpful guidance and resources for individuals in Ecuador affected by power outages. It is straightforward and informative, ensuring clarity for users.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to offer practical information and tips to help individuals and families in Ecuador cope with power outages. This includes:

  • Guides on protecting electronic devices during outages
  • Energy-saving strategies
  • Schedules and links to official outage timing resources
  • Community support and preparation tips

Scenarios

Key questions the site will address:

  • How can I protect my appliances during power outages?
  • Where can I find the schedule for outages in my area?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #1E88E5 (Blue) - Used for headings, buttons, and links to signify trust and reliability.
  • Secondary Color: #FFC107 (Amber) - Used for accents, alerts, and calls to action to grab attention.
  • Background Color: #FFFFFF (White) - Provides a clean and readable canvas.
  • Text Color: #333333 (Dark Gray) - Used for body text to ensure readability.

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Roboto", sans-serif - Used for all headings to maintain a modern and clean appearance.
  • Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif - Used for body text for enhanced readability.
  • Special Sections: "Courier New", monospace - Used sparingly for technical guides or emphasis.
